How to take a screenshot

This tutorial is to teach you how to take a screenshot to capture an image of your computer screen

On Windows

  • Navigate to the page you want to take a screenshot
  • On windows press: Windows Key+Shift+S
  • Your screenshot will now be copied to your clipboard

On Mac

  • Navigate to the page you want to take a screenshot
  • On windows press: Command+Shift+3
  • Your screenshot will now be copied to your clipboard
